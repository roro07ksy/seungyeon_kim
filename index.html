Robotic hand design Telemanipulation Humanoid AI

Seungyeon Kim received the B.S. degree from the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Seoul National University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2015, and the Ph.D. degree in robotics from the Department of Intelligent Convergence Systems, Seoul National University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2023. From 2023 to 2024, he was a staff engineer in the Autonomous Material Development TU at the Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology(SAIT), Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. From October 2024, he has been a principal engineer at Robros Inc., Seoul, South Korea. His research interests include robotic hand design, telemanipulation, and AI-based grasping for multi-fingered robotic hands.
